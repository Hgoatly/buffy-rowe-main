MY CLIENTS FEEL TRANSFORMED AND EMPOWERED
I WOULD LOVE TO HELP YOU TOO

Basic Package

You know what you would like to achieve and feel stuck with knowing where to start, how to rebalance yourself and keep you focussed on your goals. You would like some greater understanding about what is keeping you where you are and want some support and direction to help get yourself on track to feeling well. You are optimistic and blossoming feels in reach!

Package includes:

Initial assessment, Nutritional Guidance, support with tools to enable lifestyle changes and accountability.

Comprehensive Package

You have struggled to prioritise yourself and feel like your own health and wellbeing is draining and frustrating you. You know you will benefit from focussed support and would like regular guidance for you to realign your journey and feel well and empowered to ride the inevitable waves of life.

Package includes:

Initial assessment, Nutritional Guidance, regular support and review with tools to enable lifestyle changes and accountability. You will benefit from deepening your understanding as to what is keeping you stuck and how you can build new healthy behaviours to free you to be well, confident and energised.

Ultimate Package

This is for the busy and overwhelmed, perhaps with more challenging long term health concerns or issues. You know that entrenched patterns and/or coping mechanisms are going to need added focus to shift. You will benefit from bespoke and regular support and will be guided and feel empowered. You know that without diarising your support and treatment you will remain stuck and unable to prioritise yourself as you need to. Your own health and wellbeing is disabling your love of life and enough is enough. You want to feel bright, glowing and wonderful. It’s time for change!

Package includes:

Initial assessment, Nutritional Guidance, weekly support and review and extensive coaching tools to support focus and continued motivation. You will benefit from regular access through Whatsapp and/or email support as well as weekly (at least initially) sessions. Challenging daily choices will be worked through and you will develop a comprehensive ‘took kit’ of how to navigate yourself towards your freedom and ultimate health.
