LEARN HOW TO TAKE YOUR HAPPINESS INTO YOUR OWN HANDS AND KEEP IT THERE

Allow me to introduce you to Matt Pepper; practitioner, author, speaker and mental-wellness trainer. I have known Matt since 2010 and have closely followed his work and benefitted from his wisdom ever since. I am delighted to now be an accredited practitioner of this material.

Matt wrote, ‘Happiness, the Inside Job’ in 2018. It is a comprehensive and powerful method, making happiness an accessible reality to literally everyone. More recently, Matt has produced ‘Fire Up Your Happiness’, which is an online course, based on this material.

Both Matt and I, present this method in talks and workshops. I love teaching this content to people of all ages. I use it within 1:1 coaching sessions with my own clients, as well as presenting masterclasses and interactive sessions.
