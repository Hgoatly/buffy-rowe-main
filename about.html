Do you feel unhealthy or heavy?

Perhaps you have tried many approaches with only short term or limited success. You may even doubt that you can ever feel how you would like to.

I’m Buffy Rowe, a Registered Nutritional Therapist and Health Coach. I specialise in helping my clients achieve the quality of life they have been craving.

I combine Nutritional Therapy with Health coaching and specialist training in disorder eating behaviours and obesity. In overcoming anxieties a out food and health issues, my clients find freedom from their uncomfortable symptoms, to be able to live the balanced, calm and liberated lives they have been longing for, often for many years.

I have had personal experience of living with thyroid hormone issues, distressing anxiety related IBS symptoms and have worked through unwanted disordered eating behaviours. It has been quite a journey and one that I feel proud and grateful for. It lead my to my extensive retraining and the creation of a career that I love. I am passionate my clients feeling heard, supported and empowered as they turn their health around.

With a thyroid condition that was diagnosed at a young age (teens), my personal health journey started at a young age. My symptoms started to shape my self confidence, self esteem and enjoyment of life. Fast forward to around 2014 and I decided to try a new approach, firstly for myself, and then my career. I haven’t looked back!

I was a professional freelance musician (violinist) for 14 years and am no stranger to the never ending challenges of balancing work and family and matching lifestyle with health issues. Musicians have unconventional and colourful lifestyles. Working hours are erratic and coupled with a heady mix of adrenaline, excitement, and often high anxiety. Once I had started a family, they came first, and my own health and well being trailed behind. I had no idea that it was possible to strike a fulfilling and healthy balance....
